Gameplay Basics

In Valorant, the main game mode is a 5v5 tactical shooter where one team attacks and the other defends. The attacking team’s objective is to plant a bomb, known as the Spike, at one of the bomb sites. The defending team must prevent the Spike from being planted or defuse it if it is. Each round, players earn currency based on their performance, which they can spend on weapons, shields, and abilities.
